## A note on DOC-06 and DOC-07

Both are re-scoped from the original document (revisions **R-6**, **R-7**): 245 environment variables
rather than "the .env file", and ~70 tables rather than 36. Neither can be done exhaustively to the
depth the original asks for in one day, which is why both use a **tiered** approach — full treatment
for what matters, one-line coverage for the long tail, and generated tables where generation is
honest.

If Day 19 is going to overrun, the right compression is to cut Tier 3 depth, **not** Tier 1. Tier 1 is
the variables and tables that cause outages and data loss. That is the part a new engineer needs.
