## TKT-016 · Split `call_guard.py` (1,452 lines)

**Maps to:** original task 35 (fix 24) · **Est:** 3h · **Status:** ⬜
**Shared split rules:** see `2026-08-03_DAY-12.md` — they apply here in full.

### Goal — done means
`backend/app/domain/services/call_guard.py` is decomposed into modules under 500 lines, behaviour
identical, gate green.

### Why this file, and why carefully
`CallGuard` is the component that decides whether a call is **allowed to be placed**: DNC, rate
limits, concurrency, geography, business hours, subscription status. A behaviour change here does not
cause a crash — it causes an illegal call, or a campaign that silently dials nobody.

Both failure modes have already happened on this project. A stack of guard blockers once caused a
691-lead campaign to place **zero** calls, and it took a dedicated investigation to find. The
subscription check gained a 60-second Redis cache (fail-through-to-DB) as part of fix 20. **DNC
caching was deliberately rejected** for compliance: a number added to DNC must never be dialable from
a stale cache.

### Suggested seams (verify first)
| Module | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| `call_guard/dnc.py` | DNC lookup — **uncached, deliberately** |
| `call_guard/rate_limits.py` | Velocity + abuse limits |
| `call_guard/concurrency.py` | Global and per-tenant concurrency leases |
| `call_guard/schedule.py` | Business hours, timezone, geography |
| `call_guard/subscription.py` | Subscription/quota check + its 60s cache |
| `call_guard/decision.py` | Composition of the above into one outcome |

### Do
- Preserve **decision order and short-circuit semantics exactly.** Which check runs first determines
  which reason a blocked call is attributed to, and that is what operators read.
- Carry the "do not cache DNC" comment into `call_guard/dnc.py` verbatim. It is the only thing
  standing between a future optimiser and a compliance breach.
- Keep `call_guard_decisions` telemetry writing exactly what it wrote before.

### Don't
- ❌ Do not reorder checks.
- ❌ Do not add caching to anything that was not cached.
- ❌ Do not "simplify" a guard that looks redundant. Guards look redundant right up until the day
  they are the only thing that fired.

### Checklist
- [ ] Seams verified against the file
- [ ] Every module < 500 lines
- [ ] Decision order and short-circuits preserved
- [ ] DNC remains uncached; comment carried over verbatim
- [ ] Subscription 60s cache behaviour unchanged, still fails through to DB
- [ ] `call_guard_decisions` telemetry unchanged
- [ ] Full gate ≥ baseline
- [ ] AST test green, allowlist unchanged

- [ ] Peer-reviewed
